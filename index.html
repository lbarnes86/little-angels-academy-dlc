About Little Angel's Academy

Daycare and Learning Center

Little Angel's Academy is owned and operated by A'Kia Q Reese who is a native of Bassfield, MS, a graduate of Bassfield High School and William Carey University. A'Kia is a proud mother of three boys and a godmother of 1 girl.

Having several years of experience with childcare and working with adolescents, it a huge passion to bring resources such as a safe and adequate childcare center to the Jefferson Davis County community. While aware of the important need for a childcare program in our county, I take great pleasure in being able to bring a positive contribution to the communty that laid a great foundation for me. I look forward to working with the families of Jefferson Davis County!

B.S. Psychology, M.S. Child and Adolescent Therapy

Akia Reese

-owner/director
